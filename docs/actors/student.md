# Student Actor

## Description
A Student is an end user who enrolls in programs, attends sessions,
completes assignments, and receives certificates.

## Preconditions
- Student must be registered and verified
- Student must be logged in
- Student may or may not have active subscriptions

## Permissions
- View published programs
- Purchase programs
- Attend live sessions
- Submit assignments
- View certificates

## Restrictions
- Cannot access unpublished programs
- Cannot access content without enrollment
